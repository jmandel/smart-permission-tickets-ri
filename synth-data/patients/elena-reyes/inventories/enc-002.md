## Clinical Procedures / Test Procedures (Documented as Occurring)

### 3-Hour Oral Glucose Tolerance Test (OGTT) — Diagnostic Procedure
- **SNOMED Code:** 271533009 (Glucose tolerance test) or 165844004 (Glucose tolerance test 3-hour post glucose)
- **CPT Code:** 82951 (Glucose tolerance test [glucose content of specimen], more than 2 specimens) or 82952 (Glucose tolerance test 1 hour)
- **Glucose Load:** 100 grams of glucose in liquid form
- **Patient Preparation:** Overnight fast (≥8 hours); NPO before collection
- **Timing:** Four blood draws:
  - Fasting (0 minutes) — before glucose consumption
  - 1 hour post-load
  - 2 hours post-load
  - 3 hours post-load
- **Method:** Patient drinks glucose beverage; blood collected via venipuncture at specified intervals
- **Diagnostic Criteria Applied:** Carpenter-Coustan criteria (4 thresholds; ≥2 abnormal = diagnosis)
- **Results:** All 4 values abnormal → diagnostic for gestational diabetes mellitus
- **Provider Ordering:** Dr. Carmen Villanueva, MD
- **Date Performed:** 2022-01-10
- **Status:** Completed
